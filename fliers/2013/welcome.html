Welcome to SeaGL 2013

Welcome to Seattle's newest grassroots technical conference. We are dedicated to spreading awareness and knowledge about the GNU/Linux ecosystem, Free Software, and the Open Source movement.

Our goal for SeaGL is to produce an event which is both enjoyable and informative for all ranges of skill and experience. We're starting small this year but have managed to pull together a stellar lineup of volunteer presenters, and we hope that you have a great time while you're here.

 

There is no cost to attend.
