Registration

Early bird registration deadline is 15th August 2017. Please note that at least one author per accepted paper must be registered by 15th August 2017 in order for the paper to be published. If an author registers for only one paper, he/she needs to have full registration to cover the paper; if an author registers for more than one paper, one paper needs to be registered with full price and the other papers can be registered at half price. All registrations include access to all tutorial sessions, tea/coffee breaks, conference materials, and one LNCS conference proceedings on USB flash drive.
