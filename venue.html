Venue, Accommodation and Transportation

会场 (Venue)

南方科技大学图书馆报告厅 110 和 111 (Lecture Hall 110 and 111, Library, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Shenzhen, China)

交通 (Transportation)

  • 地铁(Subway)：地铁5号线塘朗站，从C出口出，向北步行500米即到学校正门（1号门）（Take the Subway Line #5 and get off at the Tang Lang Station, SUSTech (conference venue) is 500 meter away on the north.）
  • 公交车 (Bus)：坐 M369，43，74，或81路公交车，南方科技大学站下 （take the Bus # M369，43，74 or 81,and get off at the SUSTech Station.）
  • 高铁（High-speed Train）：到深圳北站，出站后，坐公交车3站路到南方科技大学站下(take the high-speed train to Shenzhen North Station, and then take the bus and get off at the SUSTech Station)
  • 飞机(Airline)：到深圳宝安机场，坐地铁11号线，转5号线到地铁塘朗站下(fly to Shenzhen Baoan International Airport, then take Subway Line #11, transfer to Subway Line #5, and get off at Tang Lang Station)。
  • 学校地址(SUSTech Address)：深圳市南山区西丽学苑大道1088号(1088 Xueyuan Avenue, Xili, Nanshan District, Shenzhen, China)
